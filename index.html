Visit Lahore

Enjoy the culture and hertiage of the oldest city in the world, dating back between the 1st and 7th centuries A.D!

Top three activities to do in Lahore:

Visit Old Lahore Venture out into one of the oldest cities in the world! See the ancient buildings and meet residents living there for years!

Arcades and Games Visit Playdium, where you can play many arcade games and enjoy indoor rides like the bull ride, the spinning tornado, and bumper cars!

Water Park Visit the huge waterpark and go on the crazy water slides, chill in the river, or have a blast in the wave pool! Other, non-water, activities are also available like go-karting, horse riding, and so much more!
